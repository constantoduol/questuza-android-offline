Creating a Service Product

To create a product :

  1. Enter a unique name for the product
  2. You can then optionally enter the product type
  3. Enter the cost of selling each unit of your product
  4. You can optionally enter the product narration
  5. Click on the Create Product button

Updating a product

To update a product:

  1. Search for the product under 'Search Products'
  2. Select the product you want to update from the drop down by clicking on it
  3. Edit any values that you want to update
  4. Click on the 'Update Product' button

Deleting a product

To delete a product:

  1. Search for the product under 'Search Products'
  2. Select the product you want to delete from the drop down by clicking on it
  3. Click on the 'Delete Product' button
