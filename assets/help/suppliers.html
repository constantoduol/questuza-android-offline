Creating a Supplier

To create a supplier :
  1. Enter the suppliers business name
  2. All the remaining fields are optional, you can choose to enter them or not
  3. Click on the 'Create Supplier' button

Updating a Supplier

To update a supplier:
  1. Search for the supplier under 'Search Suppliers'
  2. Select the supplier you want to update from the drop down by clicking on the supplier's name
  3. Edit any values that you want to update
  4. Click on the 'Update Supplier' button

Deleting a supplier

  1. Search for the supplier under 'Search Suppliers'
  2. Select the supplier you want to delete from the drop down by clicking on the supplier's name
  3. Click on the 'Delete Supplier' button

Viewing all users

To view all users created under your business click on the blue search button
