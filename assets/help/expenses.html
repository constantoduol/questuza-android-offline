Adding Incomes/Expenses

To add an Income/Expense :
  1. Select income/expense from the type drop down
  2. Enter a name for your income or expense
  3. Enter an amount for your income or expense
  4. Click on the 'Add' button and on the prompt, click the 'Ok' button to complete

Generating profit and loss

A profit and loss is generated for a specified time period.Select the beginning date and the end date and click on the 'Profit and Loss' button. The profit and loss statement contains various important values such as:
  1. Cost of Sales : This is the value of goods sold at selling price. It is the main form of revenue
  2. Opening Stock : This is the value of goods in stock at the beginning of the specified time period
  3. Purchases : This is the value of goods purchased during the specified time period
  4. Gross Profit : This is the profit/margin before considering other expenses or incomes
  5. Expenses : This is the value of other running costs such as wages, electricity etc.
  6. Incomes : This is the value of other sources of revenue apart from sales such as royalties etc.
  7. Net Profit : This is the profit after considering all other expenses and incomes
