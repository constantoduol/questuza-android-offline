Creating a Business

A business represents an organization dealing in goods or services, to create a business. The user creating the business is listed as the business owner, this cannot be changed after creation. Businesses are billed on a monthly basis depending on usage.
  1. Enter the official name of your business as per your business licence or trading certificate
  2. Enter all the remaining required details, company website is optional
  3. Select an appropriate business category depending on your business
  4. Enter a short description describing what you do as a business
  5. Click on the 'Create Business' button
  6. You will be prompted to sign in again

Updating a Business

To update a business:
  1. Edit the business details to what you require
  2. Click on the 'Update Business' button
  3. When prompted about tracking stock click ok to track stock or cancel not to track stock
  4. You will be prompted to sign in again

Deleting a Business

This is only applicable if you have more than one business
  1. Click on the 'Delete Business' button
  2. You will be prompted to sign in again

Business settings

To view or edit business settings click on the 'Settings' button. You can toggle the settings as per your requirements and then click on the 'Save' button.
  1. Enable undo sales for sales persons : this empowers sales people to be able to undo sales without an administrators intervention.
  2. Add tax as an expense to profit and loss : this makes taxes automatically calculated by the system to be added on the profit and loss statement
  3. Add commission as an expense to profit and loss : this makes commissions automatically calculated by the system to be added on the profit and loss statement
  4. Track stock movement : this is only applicable for businesses that are marked as services, it enables tracking of stock for such a business
