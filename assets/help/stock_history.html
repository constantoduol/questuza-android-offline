Generating Reports

Reports can be generated based on a variety of filters. To generate a report toggle the filters as per your requirements and then click on the 'Generate Report' button. There are four types of reports that can be generated :
  1. Stock : This report shows the movement of stock on the system such as sales, new stock, stock returned etc.
  2. Commission : This report shows the commissions earned by users on the system
  3. Tax : This report shows VAT payable by the business
  4. Supplier : This report gives a record of dealings with suppliers
