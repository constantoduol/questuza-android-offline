Selling a product

To sell a product:
  1. Search for the product under Search Products by clicking in the search area or using your keyboard by pressing Shift+F
  2. Select the product you want by pressing the enter key or by clicking on it
  3. Adjust the quantity you wish to sell, if you wish to restart the sale click on the 'Clear Sale' button or using your keyboard press Shift+C
  4. Click on the 'Commit Sale' button to complete the sale or use the keyboard shortcut Shift+Enter
  5. When prompted whether to commit the transaction, press the enter key, the transaction will then be committed.

Keyboard shortcuts

  1. Shift + F : Use this to focus on the search field
  2. Shift + C : Use this to clear the current sale
  3. Shift + Enter : Use this to commit the transaction
  4. Shift + F1 : Use this to show the current page's help
